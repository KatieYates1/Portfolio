About Me:

I am a 2nd year Game Design and Development student at Rochester Institute of Technology. With a strong passion for the arts and helping out my friends, I have work on many games including genres like horror, narrative, and 2D minigames. I cant wait to continue learning and growing my skills in school and in the industry.

Flower Shop: Business is Blooming, starting with a group of three in WICHacks 2025. It includes me, Bella Miller and Abby Rochelle. It's a game we started with not really any knowledge of making games since it was only our first year at RIT. Since then we have been working on making it a better game and hopefully apply to RIT’s Magic Maker program to get the game shipped! As the lead designer for the game, I have sketched and created many mockups of characters and environments. I also help design the game mechanics and how we are going to take the story. It's been a really fun experience and I can’t wait to continue to develop the game!
